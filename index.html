Networks of Wellington

Connecting with people is easy around Wellington with the wide range of networks available. Wellingtons Network industry is complied of different companies and networks connecting people from around the world. Wellington City has various types of technology helping you to connect from Underground cables to Antennas - all helping us to connect.


VARIOUS ANTENNAS

An interface that helps transmit waves through space and electric currents. Usually used with a transmitter or receiver.


SATELLITE DISH

A satellite dish is a dish-shaped type of parabolic antenna designed to receive or transmit information. Most commonly used by consumers to receive direct-broadcasts.


ANTENNA

An antenna is an array of conductors (elements), electrically connected to the receiver or transmitter. During transmission, the oscillating current applied to the antenna by a transmitter creates an oscillating electric field and magnetic field around the antenna elements. These time-varying fields radiate energy away from the antenna into space.


TRAFFIC SIGNAL CONTROLLERS

The traffic signals communicate via radio waves about traffic volumes and other parameters at their respective locations.




STREET MARKINGS

These are left by contractor or construction companies so they can mark out the location of underground cables so the contractors knows to watch out for them. These street markings can be seen spray painted on sidewalks in various colours.

TELECOMMUNICATION CABLES

Underground - External telephone distribution cables are designed for direct burial installation in conduit, or overhead suspension between power lines. Particularly suited where security against moisture ingress is required (underground only).
